Common Issues & Fixes

Facing a glitch? Check these common scenarios before contacting support:

1. Login/Signup Failure

Ensure your email format is correct and your internet connection is stable. If the database is down, try again in 5 minutes.

2. Response Not Generating

If the AI doesn't reply, the API limit may have been reached. Refresh the page or clear your browser cache.

3. Data Not Saving

Ensure you are logged in. If you are in "Guest Mode," your progress will not be saved to the database.

4. Page Layout Issues

ByteBuddy AI works best on Chrome or Edge. Ensure your browser is updated to the latest version.
